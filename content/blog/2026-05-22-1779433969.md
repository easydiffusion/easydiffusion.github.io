Just completed another round of fixing support issues in Easy Diffusion. Today's evidence continues to convince me to move Easy Diffusion away from the Python ecosystem for AI inference on end-user PCs. And definitely to stay away from conda, it's so leaky. Both are excellent in their own right (especially for training and research), but I don't think they make sense for end-user inference.

Easy Diffusion's v4 full rollout (which drops Python, torch and conda) can't come soon enough.
